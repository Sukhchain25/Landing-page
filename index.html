Our Process

How We Work

From idea to doorstep, our process is built for global buyers who need reliability, customization, and consistent quality in every shipment.

1

Initial Consultation & Product Briefing

We begin by understanding your business, product goals, and market dynamics. Whether you're sourcing cushions for a retail chain, relief blankets for humanitarian projects, or custom-designed rugs, we ask the right questions to ensure alignment. We also guide you through fabric choices, fill materials, dimensions, target price points, certifications required, and packaging expectations — all tailored for your audience and destination country.

2

Prototype Development & Sample Approval

Once the specs are finalized, we develop product samples that match your brief to the finest detail — stitching, texture, size, and color accuracy. We ship these samples to you for hands-on evaluation. Feedback is welcomed and encouraged. Need tighter stitching? Softer fill? Label adjustments? No problem. We iterate till you sign off on a version you'd confidently scale and sell. This phase eliminates guesswork before mass production.

3

Production with Real-Time Quality Checks

Once approved, your order moves into production. Our facilities combine skilled artisanship with industrial efficiency. Every batch goes through multiple quality control checks — raw material verification, cutting precision, stitching strength, visual inspection, and weight/size conformity. Production timelines are transparently shared with you, and we keep you updated at each milestone. We believe that great products come from a controlled and traceable process — not just machines.

4

Packaging, Documentation & Global Shipping

After final QC, your products are packed with export-grade materials to ensure safe delivery across long distances. We handle all necessary export documentation including invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, fumigation (if required), and coordinate with your freight partner or ours. Whether it's FOB or door delivery, we ensure your goods arrive intact, on time, and hassle-free. We also share post-shipment tracking and remain in touch until final delivery.
